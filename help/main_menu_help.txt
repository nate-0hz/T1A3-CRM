CRaM Main Menu
==============

You are at the main menu of CRaM.

(1) - To view or search through your contacts, type 1 then press enter.
(2) - To add a new contact to your CRaM, type 2 then press enter.
(3) - To edit existing contacts, type 3 then press enter.
(9) - You know what this does. Entering 9 brings up help information. Like this. Magic.
(0) - To quit CRaM and return to your terminal, type 0 then press enter.

Press any key to return to the menu.
