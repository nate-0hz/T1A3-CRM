CRaM View Menu
==============

You are at the view menu of CRaM.

(1) - View all contacts. You can order them by column in ascending order.
(2) - Search through CRaM for a contact that contains the search term you enter.
(9) - You know what this does. Entering 9 brings up help information. Like this. Magic.
(0) - To go back to the main menu, type 0 then press enter.
